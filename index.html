Built for real church services

Every feature designed by and for church media teams who need reliability on Sunday morning.

Dual-Path Detection

Fast regex catches explicit references like "Romans 8:28" in under 600ms. A local LLM detects thematic allusions like "all things work together" in parallel.

Zero Hallucinations

Every AI-detected reference is validated against a real Bible database before display. If it's not a real verse, it never reaches your screen.

Accent-Aware

Powered by Whisper with Nigerian English accent tuning. Works with diverse speaking styles and accents from around the world.

Compact Overlay

Toggle an always-on-top transparent overlay showing recent scripture detections. Perfect for second screens or projector setups.

Multiple Translations

Ships with KJV. Drop in EasyWorship Bible files for NIV, ESV, NKJV, and more — they're instantly available.

Operator Controls

Confirm, dismiss, copy, or pin each detection. Your team stays in control — AI suggests, humans decide what goes on screen.

How it works

From spoken word to displayed scripture in under a second.

1

Listen

Kerygma captures audio from your computer's microphone or audio interface. Whisper AI transcribes speech in real-time with high accuracy.

2

Detect

Two AI paths run simultaneously — a fast regex parser spots explicit references instantly, while a local LLM identifies thematic quotes and allusions.

Fast Path <600ms
Smart Path ~5s
3

Validate

Every detected reference is checked against a real Bible database. No hallucinations, no made-up verses — only verified scripture reaches your operator.

4

Display

Verified verses appear as cards in the desktop app. Operators can confirm, copy to clipboard, pin important verses, or dismiss false positives.
